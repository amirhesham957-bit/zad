# Zad Design System

## Vision
Zad is a premium financial and provisions tracking app. The aesthetic is "Cyber-Luxe" – combining deep slate backgrounds with vibrant indigo accents and sophisticated glassmorphism.

## Colors
- **Background**: #0F172A (Deep Slate)
- **Primary**: #6366F1 (Vibrant Indigo)
- **Secondary**: #1E293B (Slate 800)
- **Accent**: #818CF8 (Soft Indigo)
- **Warning**: #EF4444 (Rose Red)

## Components
- **Glass Card**: Background #1E293B with 80% opacity, backdrop blur 20px, border 1px solid #FFFFFF1A.
- **Action Button**: Full pill shape, gradient from #818CF8 to #6366F1, box shadow glow.
- **Inventory Item**: Slate 800 background, rounded 16px, subtle hover state.

## Typography
- **Primary**: Inter
- **Headings**: Semi-bold, tight letter spacing.
- **Body**: Regular, legible.
